Technolgy as a Solution in ‘New Normal’ Era Covid-19

The current Covid-19 pandemic has an impact in the form of limited human movement due to the necessity to keep a distance and avoid the crowd. This will certainly limit the various activities in companies in various fields, such as government, education, business, economics, and health.
